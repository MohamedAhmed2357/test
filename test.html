Welcome to the Astronomy Competition 2024! This event is designed for students, amateur astronomers, and space enthusiasts to test their knowledge, problem-solving skills, and passion for the cosmos. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced stargazer, this competition offers a fun and challenging way to explore the wonders of the universe.

Participants will engage in quizzes, observation challenges, and theoretical problems covering various astronomical topics. Join us for an exciting journey through the stars!

üìú Competition Rules

  • Eligibility: Open to individuals aged 12 and above. Teams of up to 3 members are allowed.
  • Format:
    • Preliminary Round: Online quiz (multiple-choice & short-answer questions).
    • Final Round: Practical observation tasks & in-depth problem-solving (held in-person or via video call).
  • Scoring: Points awarded for accuracy and speed. The top 3 highest scorers advance to the finals.
  • Code of Conduct: No external help or unauthorized resources allowed. Violations lead to disqualification.
  • Equipment: For observation rounds, basic tools like binoculars or telescopes may be required (details provided in advance).

üî≠ Topics Covered

The competition will include questions and tasks related to:

  • Solar System: Planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.
  • Stars & Galaxies: Stellar life cycles, black holes, Milky Way, and distant galaxies.
  • Cosmology: Big Bang theory, dark matter, and the expanding universe.
  • Astrobiology: Search for extraterrestrial life and habitable exoplanets.
  • Space Exploration: Missions, telescopes (Hubble, JWST), and future projects.
  • Observational Astronomy: Constellations, celestial navigation, and telescope use.
